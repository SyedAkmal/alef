Aléf Homes

Alef Constructions is a wholly owned subsidiary of Alef Homes Group, A real-estate developer company with a decade experience of real estate development and construction.

To leverage the internal experience and to diversify the business, Alef Constructions has been spin off as independent Contracting Company to pursue external construction contracts as well back-end service provider to the parent group.

Alef Constructions will be a key pillar and profitability driver for the Group, successfully providing construction and development work for internal & external clients.

Vision

Development company committed to total customer satisfaction and enhancing shareholder's value, by building on our strengths - innovative designs, superlative quality of material, cutting-edge technology, timely completion and demonstrating the highest standards of workmanship.

Mission

To strengthen our position as a market leader in Infrastructure Construction & Real Estate Development Projects.
